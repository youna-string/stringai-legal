String AI — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

시행일: 2026년 7월 6일

String AI is an AI-powered practice app for violin, viola, and cello. This policy explains what we collect, what we never collect, and the rights you have. Questions: celloluv92@gmail.com.

1. How we keep your data safe

Your phone does the heavy lifting. Pitch, rhythm, tone, dynamics, and posture are all analyzed locally on your device. Only anonymized scores (numbers) ever leave your phone. No raw audio or video is uploaded to our servers — ever. Your music stays yours.

2. What we collect

Account: email and a chosen display name. Practice metrics: per-axis scores, mission attempts, session duration, instrument and level. Schedule preferences: pieces you picked and how long you plan to practice. That's it.

3. What we never collect

Raw audio recordings. Raw video frames. Your real name, address, phone, school, or any other identifier beyond email. We do not sell or rent any data to third parties — ever.

4. AI coaching (OpenAI)

We use OpenAI (GPT-4o API) to give you coaching. Only anonymized numbers and the piece title (public information) are sent — never your raw playing, your name, or your email. By default, the OpenAI API does not use the data you send to train its models. Data is retained for at most 30 days for abuse monitoring and then deleted (unless we are legally required to keep it).

5. Children and parents (under 14)

Students under 14 require a parent or guardian's explicit consent before any data is collected. Parents can view, export, or delete their child's data at any time from Settings. We comply with COPPA (US, age 13) and PIPA (Korea, age 14), using the stricter 14-year threshold globally.

6. Your rights

You can: (1) access and review what's stored about you, (2) export all your data anytime (JSON download in Settings) — the right to data portability, (3) correct inaccurate data (rectification), (4) delete your account permanently (data wiped within 30 days) — the right to erasure, (5) withdraw analytics consent without losing core features, and (6) object to or restrict certain processing. These rights are guaranteed by GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and PIPA (Korea). You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority — in the EU/EEA, your local supervisory authority; in the Republic of Korea, the Personal Information Protection Commission (PIPC).

7. Data retention

Active account: practice metrics are kept as long as you keep your account. Account deletion: all personal data wiped within 30 days; anonymized aggregate metrics may be kept for product improvement (no link back to you). The only exception is the limited transaction records we are legally required to retain (for example, tax and e-commerce record-keeping laws), which are kept for the minimum period the law requires and then deleted.

8. Service providers and international data transfer

We rely on a small number of trusted service providers (processors) who handle data on our behalf under contract, solely to run the service: Supabase (secure database, authentication, and hosting of your account and anonymized practice metrics) and OpenAI (GPT-4o API, which generates coaching from anonymized numbers only). These providers are located in the United States, so practice metrics may be processed there. By using the app you consent to this transfer. Where data is transferred outside your region (for example, from the EEA or Korea to the United States), we rely on appropriate legal safeguards such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses. All transfers are protected with TLS encryption, and data is encrypted at rest. OpenAI's API platform is SOC 2 Type II certified. We do not sell or share your data with anyone beyond these processors.

9. Posture (biometric) data

When the camera analyzes posture and bowing, your phone computes body-position (posture) measurements. Because these may qualify as biometric information, we disclose them separately. What: posture angles derived from body keypoints (positions of shoulders, arms, wrists, etc.). Why: solely for posture and bow-technique coaching. Where: processed entirely on your phone (offline) — neither the raw video nor the body keypoints are ever transmitted off the device. Retention & destruction: each frame is discarded immediately after analysis and is never stored permanently; only the session score (numbers) remains. We never sell or share this data with third parties. (Notice, consent, and destruction policy under Illinois BIPA, Texas CUBI, Washington's biometric law, and Korea's PIPA. Granting camera permission constitutes your consent to this collection.)

10. Subscriptions and payments

String AI offers auto-renewing monthly subscriptions purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple or Google — we never receive or store your credit-card, bank, or billing details. To activate and verify your subscription, we receive a transaction identifier and the product you purchased from the store, and store it linked to your account. We use this only to grant your membership, restore your purchases, and meet tax and accounting obligations. Renewal, cancellation, and refund terms are described in our Terms of Service.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves or as the law requires. When we make a material change (for example, collecting a new type of data or adding a new processor), we will revise the effective date at the top of this page and, where required, ask affected students or parents to review and re-consent inside the app. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.

12. Who we are and how to contact us

String AI is the data controller responsible for your personal data. For any privacy request — access, export, correction, deletion, withdrawal of consent, or a complaint — contact our privacy officer at celloluv92@gmail.com. We respond within 7 days. For urgent privacy issues (suspected data breach, parental concerns), please mark the subject line as PRIVACY URGENT. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may also contact your data protection authority (in the EU/EEA, your local supervisory authority; in the Republic of Korea, the Personal Information Protection Commission and the Personal Information Dispute Mediation Committee).

학생의 폰이 모든 분석을 직접 처리합니다. 음정, 리듬, 음색, 다이나믹, 자세 — 전부 폰 안에서 계산됩니다. 익명화된 점수(숫자)만 서버로 전송됩니다. 원본 오디오나 비디오는 절대 외부로 나가지 않습니다. 학생의 음악은 학생의 것입니다.

2. 수집하는 데이터

계정: 이메일과 표시 이름. 연습 데이터: 5축 점수, 미션 시도, 세션 시간, 악기, 레벨. 스케줄: 선택한 곡과 연습 기간. 그것이 전부입니다.

3. 절대 수집하지 않는 데이터

원본 오디오 녹음. 원본 비디오 프레임. 실명, 주소, 전화번호, 학교 — 이메일 외의 신원 정보. 제3자에게 판매하거나 임대하지 않습니다 — 절대로.

4. AI 코칭 (OpenAI)

코칭은 OpenAI (GPT-4o API) 가 진행합니다. 익명화된 숫자와 곡 제목(공개 정보) 만 전송되며, 원본 연주·이름·이메일은 절대 전송되지 않습니다. OpenAI API 는 기본적으로 전송된 데이터를 모델 학습에 사용하지 않습니다. 데이터는 남용 방지 목적으로 최대 30일간만 보관된 뒤 삭제됩니다 (법적 보관 의무가 있는 경우 제외).

5. 14세 미만 학생과 학부모

14세 미만 학생의 경우 학부모 또는 보호자의 명시적 동의 후 데이터 수집이 시작됩니다. 학부모는 Settings 에서 자녀의 데이터 열람, 다운로드, 삭제가 가능합니다. COPPA (미국, 13세) 와 PIPA (한국, 14세) 중 더 엄격한 14세 기준을 글로벌하게 적용합니다.

6. 학생의 권리

(1) 저장된 데이터 열람, (2) 전 데이터 export (Settings 에서 JSON 다운로드 — 데이터 이동권), (3) 부정확한 데이터 정정, (4) 계정 영구 삭제 (30일 이내 모든 데이터 제거 — 삭제권/잊힐 권리), (5) 분석 동의 철회 (핵심 기능 유지), (6) 특정 처리에 대한 반대·제한. GDPR (EU), CCPA (캘리포니아), PIPA (한국) 의 권리가 보장됩니다. 또한 감독기관에 민원을 제기할 권리가 있습니다 — EU/EEA 는 거주지 감독기관, 대한민국은 개인정보보호위원회(PIPC).

7. 데이터 보관 기간

활성 계정: 계정이 유지되는 동안 연습 데이터를 보관합니다. 계정 삭제 시: 30일 이내 모든 개인 데이터가 삭제됩니다. 익명화된 집계 데이터는 제품 개선 목적으로 유지될 수 있으며, 학생의 신원으로 역추적이 불가능합니다. 유일한 예외는 법령상 보관이 의무화된 최소한의 거래 기록(예: 세법·전자상거래법상 기록 보존 의무)으로, 법이 정한 최소 기간만 보관한 뒤 삭제합니다.
